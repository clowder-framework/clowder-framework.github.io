All Paws 2021

Monday, June 21 - Wednesday, June 23, 2021

Online

Register today!

Join the Clowder community online for three days of talks, demos, and interactive sessions June 21-23, 2021. Registration is now open via the link above! Check out our initial agenda below, with further updates to come.

New in 2021:

  • Give a Project Talk: Share your research with the broader community by giving a short talk on your project and how it uses Clowder. We have a limited number of presentation slots available to showcase the variety of work being done by the Clowder community, so sign up today! You may request a presentation slot when you register via the above link. The organizing team will notify registrants whether you have received a slot, and any next steps, by the end of May.
